About Us

We are a grassroots NGO committed to empowering women and uplifting children through education, health initiatives, livelihood training, and social justice advocacy. Since 1996, we've worked tirelessly to nurture communities, promote equality, and build a better future for all.

Our Work

  • Women Empowerment: Skill training, self-help groups, and leadership development.
  • Child Welfare: Health camps, education programs, and child rights awareness.
  • Livelihood Projects: Vocational courses and self-employment support.
  • Government Scheme Outreach: Awareness and assistance for central/state welfare schemes.
